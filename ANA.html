Objective

To design and develop a user friendly website to represent law firm “Arun Natarajan Associates and Advocates”. This website should include all their details so that it will be informative for a client to know about the law firm and create a brand value among the public.

Our goals during this project was

Here are the basic characteristics of low-fidelity prototyping:

• To Design a logo

• To design a website prototype

• To develop the approved prototype

Logo

The law firm needed a perfect brand logo. Since its law based firm we needed to undergo a proper research to know the user feeling, to know the baseline needs of a logo and represent the law firm so it builds trust, loyalty which is much needed value on a law firm.

The client needed more attractive logo, preferred to have law icons in it and wanted the logo to be in round. After getting the clients review we finalised this logo.

Deisgn Prototype

The client wanted to showcase their practice areas, office location and contact information so based on that we have designed a prototype.

The client liked the elements but they wanted different colours and little alterations.

Final Prototype

The final prototype is build after many iteration from the firm' side. This whole website was built using programming languages like HTML,CSS, BOOTSTRAP
