INTRODUCTION

Tindog is a website for a dating app for dogs. This website represents the product's brand value. This was created in Adobe XD and coded in HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap.

You should be familiar with human dating websites that help you find a match that meets your expectations and interests. You only need to enter your information and photos there, and you are ready to go. Tindog functions similarly to a dating service for animals.

You will obtain the applicable profiles after providing all the information required about your pet, and you may select or reject the pets with a simple click or by getting in touch with the profile owner.

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to assist dog owners in connecting with a virtual community of dog enthusiasts who can assist visiting pet parents in finding secure and pet-friendly lodging. In order for dog owners to ensure that their dogs are well cared for, Tindog carefully selects each of its hosts to ensure that they are sincere dog lovers with the necessary pet-care knowledge.

In addition, Tindog gives you the freedom to select your pet partner according to your preferences without letting you compromise on your standards. Additionally, it enables every guest to do their pet-matching in the comfort of their own home. With its special capabilities, our website has made it simpler to discover the ideal pet companion for mating.
